Sometimes the separation of page segments by space is not clear enough. Then we can add this layout element as an explicit visual separation.
{.p-lead} <!--more-->

## Syntax

```md {.left}
*** or ---
```

The horizontal rule is depicted by three asterisks or three hyphens at the beginning of a line. Itâ€™s possible to separate the characters by one space: `* * *` or `- - -`. They may be indented by up to three spaces. No other characters are allowed to follow on the same line.
